Lasagna

Description

suppose since I have recipes for Skillet Lasagna, Lasagna Soup, Lasagna Roll Ups, and Taco Lasagna, we are long overdue for me to share my easy, classic lasagna recipe. This recipe has simple ingredients but tons of flavor with a creamy ricotta mixture, savory meat sauce, and lots of mozzarella cheese. This is a great freezer meal, make-ahead recipe, and leftovers freeze and reheat super well.

Ingredients

  • Spoon meat sauce
  • 4 boiled lasagna noodles
  • 1/3 ricotta cheese
  • lightly greased casserole dish

Steps

  1. Repeat 3 times
  2. Cover and bake at 375c./30 mins.
  3. Let it rest for 15 min.
  4. Broil if desired
